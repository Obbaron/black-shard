## What this faith IS, in one breath

**Sideria** is the ancient star religion of **Sylvargenta**, the silver-forest elven kingdom that a god's blight split into the two modern silver-lands (Valle d'argento and Hoja plata). It worships the **fixed** stars — the things that never move — as guides to be steered by, and it teaches a discipline of **stillness and waiting for the right moment** while the river of souls carries everyone through an **infinite, repeating cycle**. It is the deep past the rest of the setting is a reaction against: the world that still turned, worshipped by a people the desert already destroyed.

## The cosmos [CANON]

### Two orders of thing: the fixed and the flowing

- **The fixed stars** — do not move. *Because* they do not move, you can **steer by them** (guidance upon the seas). They are the objects of the faith.
- **The souls** — do not stay. They are the **moving water** of the Nel. You navigate BY the stars; you do not become one. (Scrap any "souls become stars" — different orders entirely.)

### Theology of stillness

Virtue is **fixity-with-purpose**: hold still, hold your bearing, **wait for the right moment**, and know it when it comes because you never lost your orientation. Not passivity — the discipline of the one who waits and holds true. Stillness as *readiness*.

### The Nel, the Siderian version

The river of souls **does not flow anywhere.** It **cycles into itself, across the planes, infinitely repeating.** A closed current, an eternal return. Souls cycle forever.

> **The seam that IS the religion:** stars fixed, souls not, and **the fixed stars are how you endure being on the wheel.** You cannot get off the river. You hold your bearing on what never moves and wait for the right moment — and because the river repeats, **the moment comes around again.** Patience across eternity.

### The deep-time inversion [CANON — important placement in the setting]

The Sylvans are the one culture for whom the **turning, cyclical cosmos is simply true and simply home.** They held this *before Yavanna existed*, before the Severance stopped anything.

- **Eutaxia** fears the cycle and **stopped** it (the Severance pinned time flat).
- **The Reckoners** compute the cycle and **mourn** it.
- **Sideria** **navigates** it — neither fears nor mourns; the wheel is the sea you sail.

They are the world that still turned, worshipped by the people the desert already destroyed.

## The Dii — THE COURT OF THE STARS [CANON]

The nine fixed figures are the **Dii** (sing. *Dius*), collectively the **Court of the Unmoving** / **the Court of the Stars**. They are **not domain-gods**. They are a **council frozen mid-debate** — the constitution of Sylvargenta, fixed overhead.

**THE POLITY THE SKY MIRRORS [CANON]:** Sylvargenta was a federation of **8 groves + 1 capital (Verta)**, nine seats, ruling **collectively by debate**. Time was no object — patience, deliberation, hearing every angle *was* the mode of government (elven; the unhurried). The 9 Dii ARE that council: **Vertex** (the capital, Verta) presides as chief/arbiter and takes no side; the 8 groves are 8 standing *positions* in an eternal argument. **You steer by them because they are the whole spread of positions, fixed, never resolving, always available.**

> **Naming note [CANON]:** *Dii* = Latin "gods" (option 2: the fossil-word says "gods" but does not settle whether the Siderians meant it — evidence in the gods-vs-guides debate, not a ruling). *Argei* is reserved for the RITE (effigies cast into the Nel), not the figures.

> **Silva vs. Talea — a history of conflict [CANON].** The Wood vs. the Cutting: conservative old-growth against expansionist new-growth. Root-and-shoot as rivals, the oldest quarrel in the Court, written into the sky as two figures that never agree.

### VERTA & THE SILVERLEAF [CANON]

Verta (capital; the Dius **Vertex**, the Zenith) holds the **sacred silverleaf tree**, **around which their ancient calendar is based.** Verta = the still centre = the timekeeping tree = *vertex*, the pole the year turns around. **The calendar is a tree, not a wheel** — very Sideria.

> **[STUB] The silverleaf calendar.** How a tree keeps time (leaf-fall? silver-turning?), reckoned FROM the fixed centre at Verta. A *fixed-point* calendar, fitting the theology — contrast Eutaxia's woven lattice and the old cyclical year. Undeveloped.

### THE BURNED GROVES — the Court's live crisis [CANON]

When the forest burned (the Sundering), three groves were **entirely burned away**:

- **Silvátra** (Silva) · **Undana** (Unda) · **Limeta** (Limes) — all burned away.

**But the burned groves still hold council seats.** With their lands gone, their seats are usually filled by **others from Verta** — so Vertex/Verta effectively votes the empty chairs.

> **THE GRIEVANCE [CANON]:** many cry this is **unfair and gives Verta too much power.** A federation of nine equals now has a capital speaking for up to four seats (its own + three burned). **The still, neutral arbiter is no longer neutral** — the chief holds a voting bloc. This is the Court's **central unresolved constitutional fracture**, and it is *visible in the sky*: three of the nine fixed figures stand for a people and a land that no longer exist, still hanging there, still counted, their voices now spoken by the centre.
>
> Resonance: burned groves = **present-but-empty seats, still counted** — another hole-that-doesn't-go-away, rhyming with Vacuus and the setting's whole pattern.

### The count, and the Zeroth schism [CANON]

| | **Orthodox Sideria** | **The Zeroth schism** |
|---|---|---|
| **Count** | **Nine Dii**, the Court | **Ten** — a 0th before the nine |
| **The void is** | **Absence.** The dark is where no star is. | **Presence.** The dark is a *thing*, and it was **first**. |
| **The dark body** | Not counted. Nothing. | **Vacuus** — the one before all, the dark of space itself. |

**VACUUS [CANON]:** the 0th Dius (schismatic). Latin *vacuus*, "empty." *The one before all.* Not a missing star — **the black the stars are printed on**, there before any light and after. Orthodoxy: mere absence. Schism: the absence has a name, and it came first.

> **On "0":** Rome had no zero; in-world it's **the one before all / the seat before the first**, not "the zeroth." "0" is our shorthand.
>
> **The absence-pattern, Sideria oldest:** burned groves (empty seats, still counted), Vacuus (schism's uncounted first) — and across faiths, Mania undayed, Menneus unnamed.

> **The rite [STUB].** Historical Roman **Argei**: rush effigies at fixed shrine-stations, cast yearly into the Tiber. Steal for the **Vigilia**: effigies at fixed stations, cast into the **Nel**. Fixed points above, river below, one ceremony. (The *rite* keeps the Argei name; the *figures* are the Dii.)

## Sylvargenta and the Sundering [CANON]

Thousands of years ago **Valle d'argento** and **Hoja plata** were **one**: a single connected **forest**, one **elven kingdom**, **Sylvargenta** ("the silver forest"), speaking **Sylvan** (the **Latin** analog).

**A god blighted the land and made the Great Desert of Wei**, cutting the forest in two. Over millennia the halves drifted into separate tongues:

| Ancient | → | Modern survivor | Area | Analog |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Sylvargenta** (one forest, elven) | | **Valle d'argento** — speaks **D'gento** | Valle d'argento | Italian |
| **Sylvan** (common tongue) | | **Ojapól** — speaks at **Hoja plata** | Hoja plata | Spanish |

**The names remember the homeland:** *Valle d'**argento*** ("valley of silver") and *Hoja **plata***/*Ojapól* ("silver leaf"). Silver in both — daughter-tongues each keeping the parent's name; itself proof of the sundering.

> - Created **the desert Yavanna later crossed to invade Vestrn** (the Severance War is fought across a god-made scar).
> - **Sundered Sylvargenta**, killing a forest the size of two nations.

## Language and transfer [CANON]

**Star-names are Sylvan fossils**, carried in fragments by the sundered survivors — like constellation names surviving in Romance from Latin. **Vertex** is one; a modern D'gento speaker no more hears "turning-pole" in it than a modern Roman hears the god in "Giovedì."

**Cultural-transfer fingerprint** — two independent proofs that the Indic-rooted Yavanna founders and ancient Sylvargenta touched before Yavanna existed:
1. **Deep cognate:** Sanskrit *vartin* (Chakravartin) and Sylvan *vertex* both from a shared "to turn" root. A lost common ancestor tongue behind both.
2. **Borrowed constellations:** Yavanna took the star-*names* without the star-*religion*.

> **[OPEN] How deep does the tree go?** Sylvan (the setting's "Latin") may be ancestral to a whole language family, not just a curiosity.

## The source: who writes `sideria.md` [CANON]

**A D'gento antiquarian** — a modern scholar of the Valle d'argento reconstructing their *own ancestors'* dead faith from fragments. Consequences for voice:

- **Distanced, philological, mournful.** Not a believer professing (contrast the Hierophancy). A descendant piecing together a religion their culture has "moved on" from.
