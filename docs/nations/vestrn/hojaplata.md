# Elven Duchy of Hojaplata

> **Status: stub.**

- The **elven** duchy of [[Vestrn]], see [[Elves]].
- Homeland of the **Ojapól** tongue (Spanish analog). The name echoes *hoja plata*, "silver leaf."
- Rendered as **great forest** at the center of the Vestrn map.
- **Descent.** Inheritors of the sundered silver-forest of **Sylvargenta** and keepers of its old star-names. The Silver kindred, cousins to the D'gento of Valle d'argento, see [[Sideria]].

## The Moons

The elves of Hojaplata named the two moons, and their names are the ones the whole west now uses:

| Moon | Cycle | Hojaplata (elven) | Vestrn | Empire |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| The greater | 27 nights | **Luna Mayor** | Luna Mayr | Monē |
| The lesser (twin) | 14 nights | **Luna Sor** | Luna Sora | Mania |

Both descend from the older [[Sideria|Sylvan]] roots *Luna Māior* ("greater moon") and *Luna Soror* ("sister moon"): **Māior → Mayor → Mayr**, and **Soror → Sor → Sora**.
