# Weilan

**Weilan** is the continent on which everything here takes place. It is named for the **Great Desert of Wei**, that cuts it in two.

- **West of the Wei** - the [[Vestrn|Duchies of Vestrn]], the home ground of this archive.
- **East of the Wei** - the [[Yavanna Empire]]: the imperial core of [[Yona]] and the northern reach of [[Dora]].
- **Across the middle** - the [[The Wall|Wall]], an ancient machine five-hundred meters high, and the vertical gate-city of [[Myridian]] that climbs it.

The Empire holds, directly or by soft power, nearly all of Weilan.
