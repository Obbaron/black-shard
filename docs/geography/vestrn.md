# Vestrn - the Western Reach

The **Duchies of Vestrn** occupy the western shore of [[Weilan]], beyond the [[Great Desert of Wei]]. Once the **Kingdom of Vestrn**, the land was shattered at the conclusion of the [[Severance War]] into duchies and baronies forbidden to unite under a king again.

**Realms:** [[Duchy of Ríkrvollr]] · [[Duchy of Sarvaald]] · [[Duchy of Kaladan]] · [[Dwarven Duchy of Nuruu]] · [[Elven Duchy of Hojaplata]] · the Baronies of Zin, Kemp, Thorne, Kros, Arlow, Dohr, Harquin.

**Named lands & landmarks:** Bosson Moor · Gorgon Hills · High Moor · Dorian Flats · Sterling Quag · Hillpoint Wood · **The Shard** (a place on the southern shore).
