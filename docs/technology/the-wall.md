# The Wall

An **ancient metal wall** running down the spine of the [[Great Desert of Wei]], roughly **half a kilometer** high. Its pistons still hiss and its turbines still turn, though **what it was built to do has been forgotten.** Whatever its purpose once was, it now does one thing: it **draws water for [[Myridian]]**, the vertical gate-city that climbs it.
