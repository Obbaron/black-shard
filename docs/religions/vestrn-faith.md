# The Faith of the West

> **Status: stub.** 

Three faiths stand over the Duchies of [[Vestrn]]:

- **Vanirism** - the old native faith, **outlawed in the [[Severance]]**. Its head is **Lysara, the War-Maiden**, who rode before the host and chose who would fall and who would stand; a thing grown out of the land itself, older than any Vestrn crown. Her feasts were the hinges of the old Vestrn year, and she has no place in the imperial calendar. Her altars are thrown down; her name is said low, or not at all. *[pantheon: the author's material, to come.]*
- **Aesirism** - the Empire-sanctioned replacement, the church of the **Aesir**, which blesses the imperial months and teaches that Lysara was a demon and the Vanir never gods. The oppression is made to wear a Vestrn face: a Vestrn god set against a Vestrn god. *[pantheon: to come.]*
- **[[Eutaxia]]** - the overlord's faith and its calendar, standing above both.
