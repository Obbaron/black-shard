# The Severance

The single act on which the present order rests, performed by [[Menneus]]. Two faces of one reordering:

- **The calendar.** The **eighth day** was struck from the year, restoring the week to the Seven of the [[Hebdomad]]. The year became **361**. See [[Eutaxian Calendar]].
- **The war.** In the **Severance War**, the [[Vestrn|Kingdom of Vestrn]] was broken into duchies and forbidden to crown a king again; in exchange its people kept their way of life, barring anti-imperial sentiment.

And from the same act: the Emperor's **immortaity**, which the faith teaches is bound to the calendar itself. Undo the Severance and the Emperor falls.

> The Hierophancy holds that the Severance was *enforced*, and that its cost is a sealed matter "not held in this hand".
