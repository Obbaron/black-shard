# Religion Creation Recipe

## 1. Culture

* Folkways (daily customs)
* Mores (core values/taboos)
* Institutions (family, state, war, trade, etc.)
* Fears & needs

## 2. Pantheon

For each major cultural value, create:

* God/domain
* Virtues
* Vices/opposition
* Symbols

## 3. Mythology

Build myths to explain and reinforce culture.

* Characters embody ideals.
* Conflict = cultural tensions.
* Victors define morality.
* Defeated define disorder, taboo, or caution.

> *Example:* God of Nobility defeats God of Freedom, legitimizing hierarchy.

## 4. Syncretism

How does the religion change?

* Borrowed gods
* Merged deities
* Reinterpreted myths
* Local variants

## 5. Ritualization

How is belief enacted?

* Rites
* Festivals
* Prayer
* Sacrifice/offering
* Life-cycle rituals

## 6. Materiality

How is belief made tangible?

* Temples/shrines
* Icons
* Sacred objects
* Clothing
* Sacred places

## 7. Lived Religion

How do ordinary people practice?

* Household worship
* Superstitions
* Local saints/spirits
* Occupational traditions
* Personal devotion
* Regional variation

## Principle

**Culture creates gods. Myths justify culture. Syncretism changes belief. Ritual enacts belief. Material culture embodies belief. Lived religion makes it real.**
